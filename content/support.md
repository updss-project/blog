## Why support UPDSS

Everything you read on this site was built by one person directing AI agents for roughly $500 in API costs. The methodology works. The architecture works. The enforcement thesis is empirically validated across 50+ releases and 6 products.

What the project needs now is a team.

### What your support funds

- **Open-sourcing the platform**: cleaning, documenting, and packaging the UPDSS codebase, vault, and methodology for public use
- **Onboarding more products**: proving the governance pattern works beyond our own projects
- **Building the tools**: the dashboard, the enforcement engine, the schema validator, the cost tracker — all need hardening for community use
- **Writing the documentation**: comprehensive guides for teams adopting AI-agent-driven development with UPDSS governance
- **Research**: continuing the R-series research that drives methodology improvements (R-088, R-093, and beyond)

### The thesis

The model choice is secondary. The *vyavastha* around the model is primary. UPDSS is the *vyavastha*. Your support helps make it available to every team building with AI agents.
