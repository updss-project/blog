Nitin Dhawan#

Writing as Muralidhar Shyam

I’ve spent much of my career designing governance systems for complex organisations. ITpreneurs, Green Dot, JustHealth, A4G Impact Collaborative — each one was a different version of the same question: how do you build structures that hold when the thing they govern becomes more capable than the structures themselves?

UPDSS is my current answer to that question, applied to the most pressing version of it: AI agents that can write production software but cannot, on their own, be trusted to ship it responsibly.

I run UPDSS from a terminal multiplexer — one orchestrator pane directing 6-9 AI agents across multiple products simultaneously. The methodology, the enforcement engine, the gate system — all of it built by AI agents under UPDSS governance. The system governs itself.
